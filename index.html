Bio

I am an incoming Ph.D. student at NYU Tandon - School of Civil Engineering, under the supervision of Eugene Vinitsky. Check out the Emerge Lab to discover what we do!
In a previous life, I obtained a M.Sc. in Actuarial Statistics, and subsequently worked at Amazon for about 8 years focusing on analytics, deep learning and deep reinforcement learning for supply chain planning in Dean Foster's group.

Outside of work I vastly enjoy climbing, gaming and go-kart racing. If I had space I'd totally start sim-racing again but I live in NYC.

Research

My research interests are in Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning (MARL) and how we can design human compatible agents that preserve better capabilities than their human counterparts in non-zero sum games. I am also always up for a discussion on Lean, formal verification, type systems (programming) and LLMs.
